About Me

Not Who You Expected

My name is Po. I am the Dragon Warrior — not because I was destined to be, but because I chose to be. I grew up among steaming pots of noodle soup, dreaming of kung fu legends while my father perfected his secret recipe. I believe that what makes us who we are is not where we start, but how fiercely we pursue what we love.

I bring to everything I do the same relentless spirit that carried me from noodle shop apprentice to Jade Palace guardian: curiosity, heart, and an absolute refusal to give up.

There are no accidents. The universe brought you here — now it's your turn to decide what you do with it.

— The Dragon Scroll (as interpreted by Po)
